Text Background
Being able to build a script from scratch is a very personalised way effectively tailoring your own responses to the sales call but at the same time not fumbling on the phone if you can’t remember one of your responses. You may know all the right things to say but saying them and remembering them at the same time is a very different issue. If you want to be you during the sales call then building your own script is the way to go. You can make the script as large or as small as you like! When you go into the build scripts bar a blank template will come up with a pitch box and the option to add as many objections as you like.
You will see that the pitch box has a number of different fonts to choose from as well as bold italics underline and you can also choose what size text you want the pitch to be. What’s also an impressive feature is the recording function at the bottom of the pitch box where you can select the icon and record your voice and you can do this for every pitch. Its very user friendly and just a matter of typing in what you want and styling the pitch how you like. You can add up to 9 objections for every template and can do so by clicking the add icon above the pitch box. The objection icons are just a standard text and doesn’t have all of the other options that the pitch box does.
Once you have typed in your pitch and all of the responses to your pitch you can then save the template under the save pitch icon. Once you have saved the template you then can click on one of the objection boxes and it will take you to another template where again you can put in your pitch and objections. There is a back icon at every new template so you can always go back to the original template which helps you to navigate through the whole script. Remember to click save pitch at after every new template otherwise you will lose the new template that you have put in place.
After you are satisfied with your script you can then go and save the script where it will take you to another screen and give you the option for a heading which you must have and a description, heading and sub heading which are optional but highly recommended. Once you have saved your script it then becomes part of your current scripts and the current script menu then applies.
